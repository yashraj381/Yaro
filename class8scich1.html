4. Write paragraphs:

a) Preparation of soil: Loosening soil for better air and nutrient supply.
b) Sowing: Placing seeds in soil at correct depth.
c) Weeding: Removal of unwanted plants.
d) Threshing: Separating grains from harvested crop.

5. Difference between fertilizers and manure:

Fertilizers are chemical and fast-acting; manure is organic and improves soil long-term.

6. Irrigation & water conservation methods:

Sprinkler System: Like artificial rain.
Drip System: Direct water to roots.

7. What if wheat is sown in kharif season?

It won’t grow well due to excess water and heat.

8. Effect of continuous cropping:

Soil loses nutrients and becomes infertile.

9. What are weeds and how to control?

Unwanted plants removed by hand or chemicals.

10. Flow chart of sugarcane production:

Preparation of soil → Ploughing → Manuring → Sowing → Irrigation → Harvesting
